What Can I Do For You?


With Tr3umphant.Designs, development for your next website and/or app is only a click away. As a personal developer with an ample amount of background experience, I can make your dream business plan, money venture, or even personal portfolio, come to life.

  • 1 First Step - Contact

    By contacting me via through this website, my email, and/or phone number, I can know that you are interested in the service. I am currently taking request for website development, (and mobile development if the concept is fairly simple).

  • 2 Second Step - Discussion/Planning

    We now need to iron out all the details of your request. To ensure you are getting the best deal for your website, we need to discuss which plan you choose, when you need the project finished, the purpose of your website, etc. I take notes of any information provided to ensure nothing is left out of the development process.

  • 3 Third Step - Development

    Now I can start creating your app. The first half of the cost of you project will be due as a deposit. In order to insure you receive your product in a timely fashion, we will have the time estimate discussed in the prior step. In this period, I will be contacting you weekly for questions/updates with your app.

  • 4 Forth Step - Completion

    At this point, your product is complete, and I will be walking you through the steps to putting your website on your own domain or on the Android/iOS market. The second half of the fee for development is due at this point as well.
